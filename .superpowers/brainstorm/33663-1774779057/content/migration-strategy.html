How should we migrate your existing content?

Your posts use Jekyll frontmatter and some have custom categories. Astro handles these differently.

A

Scripted Migration (Recommended)

I'll write a script to automatically convert Jekyll markdown files to Astro-compatible MDX/Markdown. This ensures consistency and handles frontmatter transformation (e.g., tags, categories, dates).

  • Fast for many posts
  • Reliable data mapping
  • May require manual cleanup for complex HTML
B

Selective/Manual Porting

We pick the most important posts and port them manually. Older or less relevant content stays in the old repo or is archived.

  • Cleans up old content
  • Highest quality control
  • Time-consuming
  • Risks losing historical data
